MEET THE ARTIST

Hi, my name is Alexandria but my friends call me Ali. I am from Elk River, Minnesota, but I am currently studying Digital Culture with a concentration in Design at Arizona State University's Tempe campus. I love being outdoors and going on adventures like hiking, paddle boarding, or hammocking as well as exploring and finding cute little restaurants and coffee shops.


I really enjoy painting and photography, and have recently become interested more in using digital mediums to enhance or add to photos as well as graphic design. I have also developed an interest in creating fashion. This sometimes means sketching clothing ideas and creating and other times just photographing and appreciating.

ARTIST STATEMENT

I believe that everyone you see will look different in the eyes of someone else. Through my artwork I try to explore individuality and truly capture the way I see that person. As a result this includes a lot of color to emphasize expression as well as black and white portraits that simplify the subject. I enjoy acrylic paint in more of my two-dimensional pieces as it is a smooth medium and creates serenity through the lack of friction. I also do a lot of portrait photography where I enjoy adding edits to accentuate the subject. I feel a piece is only finished when I can look at my work and feel like it has perfectly captured the personality of my subject. I have also learned, however, that sometimes what does not look good to me looks beautiful to someone else.
